# 🕵️ Imposter Game

A fun multiplayer party game that can be played on a single device. Players take turns to reveal their secret roles — one player is secretly the **Imposter** while everyone else gets a shared word. Can you figure out who doesn't know the word?

🎮 **Live Demo:** [Play Now](https://rabgyen.github.io/Imposter-Game/)

---

## How to Play

1. Add 3 or more players (supports up to 99)
2. Pass the device to each player to secretly reveal their role
3. The imposter sees **"You are the Imposter"** instead of the word
4. Discuss and vote — who doesn't know the word?
5. Reveal the imposter when ready!

---

## Features

- 🙈 Private role reveal — one device, no cheating
- 🎲 Random word from 500+ word pool
- 👥 Supports 3 to 99 players
- 📱 Works on mobile and desktop
- 🆓 Free and no account needed
